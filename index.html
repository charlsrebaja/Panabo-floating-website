About Floating Beach

Floating Cottage located near Pantalan, Panabo City Davao del Norte, is the most popular suggested place for every occasion and events, especially birthday parties. This peaceful and windy place features comfortable cottages and beautiful white sand beaches, along with a mini store for your convenience.

Not all of the floating cottages are available for rent. Many of them are also used as fish pond areas, creating a unique blend of tourism and local aquaculture. This makes Floating Beach a distinctive destination that combines hospitality with traditional livelihoods.

Whether you're planning a special celebration, looking for a peaceful getaway, or simply want to experience the charm of Panabo's coastal beauty, Floating Beach offers the perfect setting for unforgettable memories.

Location & Directions

How to Get There

Address: Floating Beach, Panabo City, Davao del Norte, Philippines

Distance from Davao City: Approximately 40-50 km (45 minutes to 1 hour drive)

Directions:

  1. From Davao City, head towards Panabo via the Davao-Cotabato Road
  2. Follow signs towards the coastal areas
  3. Head to the barangay coastal road
  4. Look for signage to Floating Beach
  5. Enjoy parking facilities and welcome areas

Best Time to Visit: Year-round, but November to May offers the best weather.
